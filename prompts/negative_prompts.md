# Negative Prompts for Packaging Generation

These negative prompts help maintain packaging accuracy and prevent unwanted changes.

---

## General Negative Prompt


blurry, low quality, distorted packaging, wrong logo,
changed typography, incorrect text, missing details,
deformed structure, unrealistic materials,
bad lighting, extra objects, artifacts


---

## Product Photography Negative Prompt


cartoon style,
illustration,
fake shadows,
oversaturated colors,
low resolution,
noise,
poor reflections,
incorrect perspective


---

## Packaging Preservation Negative Prompt


do not redesign,
do not change package shape,
do not modify graphics,
do not replace branding,
do not alter colors,
do not remove text
